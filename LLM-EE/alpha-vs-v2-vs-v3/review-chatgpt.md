**Verdict:** Strong foundation, but lacks fingerprints.

# B — Strong Professional Writer Energy

**What it does well**

* Best “newsletter/blogger” polish.
* Great examples: Amplitude, Jira, Appcues.
* Strong narrative authority.
* Nice line-level writing (“The tour works perfectly as a tour.”)

**Where it loses**

* Slightly over-engineered.
* A bit too polished in places.

**Verdict:** Excellent content marketing quality. Feels like a smart SaaS writer.

# C — Most Human and Most Believable

**What it does well**

  * “late 2022 or early 2023”
  * “twelve steps, maybe fourteen”
  * “thirty-something percent”
* These fuzzy recalls are *hugely human*.
* Best conversational texture.
* Most natural “I was there” energy.

**Where it loses**

* Slightly less sharp than D.
* Some lines wander.
* Less punchy ending.

**Verdict:** If you told me a real product consultant wrote this from experience, I’d believe you fastest.
